Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar, known as the former summer prosperity of the Old Bombay Presidency. Is now a blossoming hill station across Maharashtra. With its delightful herbage and grasslands, ancient historical landmarks, and spectacular scenery, this spot is sure to amaze you!
No trip to Mahabaleshwar is complete without a visit to "Mahabaleshwar", this stunning temple that is devoted to Lord Shiva. It is the gateway to Elephant Point, Pratapgad Fort, and is famous for its strawberry festival. Mahabaleshwar is home to over 4,000 temples and is an ideal tourist destination.
The good time to visit Mahabaleshwar is December to February.

Red fort

The Red Fort, or the Lal Qila, is situated in Old Delhi. The fort is historically, culturally, and politically an important entity for India. It is also listed as the UNESCO world heritage site.
It was constructed during the reign of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. The fort is an exclusive exhibition of Mughal architecture in Red sandstone. Visitors also enjoy the light and sound show post 6 pm every evening.
The fort timing is 9:30 am to 4:30 pm, Tuesday to Sunday. The best time to explore the place would be from October to February. See More

Jantar mantar

We have a fascination with astronomy from time immemorial. In 1724, Maharaja Jai Singh 2
concretized a 723 foot observatory situated in the heart of New Delhi.
Witness first hand an array of astronomy instruments. Come and experience how planets impact our
lives. Watch out for 4 yantras, the distinguished instruments of this wonder. The distinct orange-red
colour with its unique architecture stand out. Feel a sense of awe with a longing to go back in time.

Worlds of wonder

"The Worlds of Wonder, or WOW, is a theme park. which is situated in Noida, Uttar Pradesh's Sector 38-A. It is the ideal location for a vacation from your regular schedule.
There are amusements and activities here for individuals of all ages. There are numerous attractions, including water slides, dance floors, snack stands, and rain dances. It's the ideal location for gathering with friends and family.
